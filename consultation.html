What is a Fire Risk Assesment?

An evaluation of your premises in terms of fire safety and risk to life from fire.

Who needs a Fire Risk Assessment?

Every business needs to complete a Fire Risk Assesment.If you have five or more employees you need to record your findings.

What is it best for your Fire Risk Assessment to be completed by a professional?

An experienced Fire Risk assessor will give subjective advice, taking into account cost verses benefit and recommend actions which are reasonably practicable. Compliance ensures your piece of mind, protects your business, employees and visitors to your premises. A valid Fire Risk Assessment is also an essential requirement of your commercial insurance policy.

What are your obligations in selecting a suitable Fire Risk Assessor?

No matter who carries out the Fire Risk Assessment the duty holder or business owner retains the responsibility for ensuring the adequacy of that assessment. If employing a specialist to undertake the Fire Risk assessment, whilst you are not expected to be an expert in fire safety, you should make reasonable checks to ensure that they are competent to complete the job properly.
