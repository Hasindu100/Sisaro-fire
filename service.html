Our Services

1. Consultation

Carrying out Fire Inspections in buildings and Identification of risks related to Fire hazards, Means of Escape, Fire Installations and the other relevant operations.

A Walk-through inspection in the building and to conduct fire safety risk assessment of the facility operations, human element programs and physical protection systems, and to evaluate the overall potential exposures at buildings with respect to fire safety and related risk.

2. Training & Firefighting

Conducting In House & Online Fire Safety Awareness Training. Service of a Fire fighting & having 1000 liters of water capacity of fire Vehicle.

Provision of Fire safety leaflets, Hand books, Fire drill reports, issue of Individual & Group certificates.

3. Staff Training & Fire Drills

staff should be understand the fire precautions and the actions to be taken in the event of fire. This should include persons engaged on regular duties outside normal working hours. The aim should be to ensure that all staff receives training, based on written instructions, appropriate to their responsibilities in the event of an emergency.

  • Physical training based on physical exercise, rescue (lifting & handling casualties),
  • Fire Hydrant operation-Drill Three man & Four man (Hose laying, connecting, disconnecting, branch holding, under running, coiling of Fire delivery fire hoses),
  • First Aid Fire extinguisher training, (Identifying, operating, Inspecting, Installing)
  • Fire Alarm & Fire Detection (Inspection, Raising the alarm, Operating, Testing & Maintenance )
  • Breathing Apparatus (Donning BA, Testing, Operating)
  • Fire Pump ( Operation, Testing, Maintenance)
  • Ladder (Ascending, Descending, Pitching, Leg lock, Holding branch)

4. Security

Providing security services & Carrying out Investigations. Review of fire safety management system and emergency preparedness.
